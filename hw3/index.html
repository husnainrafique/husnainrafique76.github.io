Yum Yum Better Ice Cream, Inc

Yum Yum Better Ice Cream is located at 1219 Spring Garden St., Greensboro, NC 27403, near the University of North Carolina at Greensboro (UNCG) campus. It’s a long-standing local favorite, serving homemade ice cream and hot dogs since 1906. Their working hours are: Monday: 10:30 AM – 5:30 PM Tuesday to Friday: 10:30 AM – 9:00 PM Saturday: 10:30 AM – 5:30 PMI Sunday: Closed As for their special ice cream, Yum Yum is known for its Black Cherry and Egg Nog flavors, which are especially popular during the holiday season. The shop also offers unique treats like Butter Pecan and seasonal options

Black Walnut Ice-Cream

Black walnut ice cream features a rich, nutty flavor from the earthy, slightly bittersweet taste of black walnuts. The ice cream is creamy and smooth, with pieces of the nuts adding a crunchy texture and a unique, robust taste that sets it apart from other nut-based ice creams. Its flavor profile is deep and complex, making it a favorite for those who enjoy a more mature, refined ice cream experience.

Image credit: Yum Yum Better Ice Cream and Hot Dogs

Peanut Butter Ice-Cream

Peanut butter ice cream is a rich and creamy treat that combines the nutty flavor of peanut butter with a smooth, velvety texture. The ice cream has a slightly salty undertone, balanced by its sweetness, making it a perfect dessert for peanut butter lovers. It’s often paired with chocolate or caramel swirls for an extra indulgent twist, adding complexity to its flavor profile.

Image credit: Yum Yum Better Ice Cream and Hot Dogs

Watermelon Ice-Cream

Watermelon ice cream is a refreshing, fruity dessert known for its vibrant pink color and sweet, summery flavor that perfectly captures the essence of fresh watermelon. Its creamy texture pairs well with subtle notes of melon, making it both light and indulgent. Often enjoyed with chunks of watermelon or a hint of mint, it's a delightful treat for warm weather

Crab Cake a Classic

Crab cakes are indeed a classic, especially in Maryland where they are a beloved staple. The traditional Maryland crab cake is known for its simplicity and focus on the crab meat itself, often using minimal filler to let the flavor of the crab shine through. Key ingredients typically include lump crab meat, a bit of mayonnaise, some breadcrumbs, and seasonings like Old Bay
