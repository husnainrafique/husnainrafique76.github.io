Welcome to Granny's Pies

Granny's Pies is a family-owned bakery that has been perfecting the art of traditional pie-making for generations. Our story began with Grandma Agnes, who spent years perfecting her secret recipes and techniques to create the flakiest crusts and most delicious fillings. Today, we continue to use only the freshest ingredients and time-honored methods to craft pies that are both nostalgic and innovative.

Our Mission

At Granny's Pies, we're dedicated to sharing the joy of homemade pie with our community. We believe that pie has the power to bring people together, evoke memories, and create new traditions. That's why we're committed to using only the highest-quality ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, and to crafting each pie with love and care.

Our Pies

Our menu features a variety of sweet and savory pies, including classics like apple, cherry, and pumpkin, as well as unique creations like chocolate bourbon pecan and caramel apple crumble. We also offer a selection of quiches, pot pies, and hand pies, all made with the same attention to detail and commitment to quality.

Visit Us

We invite you to visit us at Granny's Pies, where you can sample our delicious pies, meet our friendly staff, and experience the warmth and hospitality of our family-owned bakery.
